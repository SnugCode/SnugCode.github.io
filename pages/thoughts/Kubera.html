Project thoughts

Kubera

Kubera is my local personal finance tracker: a private place to understand money habits without needing to hand that data to another service.

Why I Built It

Personal finance tools are most useful when they are honest, fast, and close to the person using them. I wanted Kubera to feel practical first: record what matters, make patterns visible, and keep the experience grounded around everyday decisions.

What I Focused On

The main idea was control. Running locally keeps the project simple to reason about and gives me room to shape the workflow around my own needs, from tracking spending to reviewing trends over time.

What I Learned

Kubera has been a useful exercise in designing around real habits instead of imaginary perfect users. Finance software can become noisy very quickly, so the challenge is choosing what to show and what to leave quiet.
